Hi there! My name is James, and I'm a 1st year university student from Canada. I live in Vaughan, Ontario with my mom, my dad, and my cat Sapphire.

I'm passionate about many different subjects, including math, science, computers and technology, music, and languages. In my spare time, I play piano; listen to, write, and arrange music; watch YouTube; read books, both fiction and non-fiction; and much more. My hobbies tend to go in cycles as my interests change.

I am attending the University of Toronto, working towards a BSc in life sciences with the goal of either completing a PhD or becoming a medical specialist. I am passionate about medical sciences and hope to use my undergraduate experience to explore different branches of the subject while gaining research experience. Some of my areas of interest currently include pharmacology, physiology, and neuroscience, especially as they relate to improving the lives of people living with chronic disabilities or mental illnesses. I also love languages and hope to one day become fluent in French.
